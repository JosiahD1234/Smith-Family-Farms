About Us

Welcome to Smith Family Farms! We are a family-owned and operated farm dedicated to providing high-quality, pasture-raised meat and farm-fresh products. Located in Southern Kentucky, we pride ourselves on raising our animals with care, feeding them non-GMO grain, and ensuring they live in a healthy and natural environment.

At Smith Family Farms, we're passionate about sustainable farming and providing the best for our community. From our pasture-raised pork and beef to our customizible meat boxes, we aim to deliver fresh, and even homemade canned goods like salsa and pickles, with delivery options to make it convenient for you.

We value our customers and the trust you place in us to feed your family. If you have more questions or want to learn more about what we offer, feel free to reach out to us.
